Summer Work and Travel

  The IntoEdVentures J-1 visa Summer Work and Travel Program is a program designed to allow 18-30 year old students, currently enrolled in college, the opportunity to live, work and travel in the USA.  College students are able to come to the United States for as long as four months during their summer break from college studies.  All students who apply to the program have the ability to ask for job placement.  The program will find a job, as well as housing, for any student who needs this assistance.  Students participating in this program have the opportunity to travel within the United States for one month after completing their work obligations.  All students participating in the program will have access to an IntoEdVentures representative at all times during their stay in the United States.
