Holy Cross High School
Mission
To educate students within a Catholic Tradition in an environment characterized by values and prayer


Features & Programs
   • Holy Cross offers an academic program in both English and
      French languages.
   • Honors Programs in Math, and English.
   • Modified courses in English, Math, Science, and History as well
      as a Learning Assistance Center that offers assistance to
      students with learning difficulties
   • An English as a Second Language program


Extracurricular Activities
Holy Cross has intramural and extracurricular programs which enable students to develop their talents and pursue their arts, athletic, and leadership interests. It has an outstanding record
in inter-school competitions. Over the years, Holy Cross High School has earned city and provincial championship in badminton, basketball, choral, cross country, curling, debate, football, soccer,
stage band, track and field, volleyball, wrestling, and yearbook events.
