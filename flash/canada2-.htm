Academic Year Program in Canada

  The Academic Year Program offers international students the opportunity to spend five to ten months living with a Canadian host family while studying at a Canadian Public or Catholic school. It is a great way to enjoy the benefits of the Canadian educational system and intensify the learning of English or French. The student stays with a host family while attending school. All families and schools are paid in this program.

  We cooperate with Public and Catholic High Schools in Ontario, New Brunswick, Quebec, Alberta, Saskatchewan, and British Columbia. The program offers high educational standards at different levels of difficulty, including advanced university preparatory courses. Exchange students can graduate if they fulfill the criteria.

  REQUIREMENTS FOR ALL CANADIAN PROGRAMS:

• Intermediate knowledge of English
• SLEP test required
• Return air ticket
• At least $300.00 CAD spending money per month
• Student must abide by rules of school and SMG

There is no deadline for applications.
