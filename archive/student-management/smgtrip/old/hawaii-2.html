Tropical sunsets and the clear blue warm waters of the Pacific Ocean make Hawaii a great choice in the middle of winter.  Included in your tour are so many different activities. 

Trip Highlights:

  • Swimming, snorkeling and Catamaran rides on fabulous Waikiki Beach
  • Visit Pearl Harbor, a pineapple plantation and an American Royal Palace
  • Hike Diamond Head Volcano and attend an authentic Hawaiian Luau
  • Tour the North Shore and its famous surf beaches

Come and experience this tropical paradise on the island of Oahu in the middle of the Pacific Ocean.  Tour the city of Honolulu and see the Iolani Palace where real kings and queens once ruled the Hawaiian Islands.  Spot dolphins and whales as you ride the waves of Waikiki on a catamaran.  Snorkel at a state park and come face to face with all types of tropical marine wildlife.  Hike to the top of Diamond Head a real volcano.  Visit Pearl Harbor and the Arizona Memorial. 

Marvel at the giant waves on the beaches of the North Shore.  Shop at the International Market in Waikiki.  Learn Hula dancing, eat authentic Hawaiian food and see an exotic show at an island Luau.  Dance the night away at a private disco party.  Warm tropical breezes, beautiful sunsets, the music of the Islands and the “Aloha Spirit” awaits you!

This is a trip not to be missed!
