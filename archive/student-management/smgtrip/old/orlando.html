The Road to Orlando - an adventure in dreams and magic!
Palm trees, white sandy beaches, and a Kingdom filled with magic! Join us on The Road to Orlando. Spend a day at Walt Disney World’s Magic Kingdom. It is a breathtaking world of fantasy. All the beloved Disney characters and classic stories come to life.

Tour Epcot Center, a place as big and colorful as your dreams. See amazing fireworks. Tour the Kennedy Space Center, America’s premier gateway to the universe. Spend free time at Universal’s City Walk. Join in the excitement at Universal Studios Theme Park. Enjoy fun in the sun at Daytona Beach!

Walt Disney World’s Magic Kingdom – A magical world with seven lands of fantasy. Visit Main Street USA, Tommorowland, Adventureland, Frontierland, and much more! Take a ride through the Haunted House. Try not to miss the grand parade down Main Street. There is so much to do. You will wish the day would never end.

Walt Disney World’s Epcot Center – Touch the past and gaze into the future in this amazing place. See Spaceship Earth. Visit the Universe of Energy, Imagination, The Land, Innovations, The Living Seas, and so much more. Discover the World Showcase, as wonderful as a giant world’s fair, all surrounding a lake. Tour the countries of Mexico, Norway, China, Japan, Italy, Germany, Morocco, United Kingdom, Canada, and The American Adventure. At the end of the day the sky will erupt with light and fire in a celebration of our world in fireworks. It’s the most magnificent show of its kind.

Treat yourself to a self-guided tour of the Kennedy Space Center, America’s gateway to the Universe. This is the world’s only launch site for the Space Shuttle.

DaytonaBeach – Step into the surf of the Atlantic ocean at this world famous beach.   Shop at the popular sea shell stores on oceanfront streets.  

Universal Studios – Go behind the scenes at the movies. Join in the action and excitement. You can soar through time with the Terminator, or chase aliens down the streets of New York. Spend free time shopping and browsing at Universal City Walk.
