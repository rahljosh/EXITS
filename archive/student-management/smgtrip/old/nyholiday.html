Christmas in New York is spectacular!  Come see one of the greatest cities in the world in all of its breathtaking holiday splendor!

This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see New York City decorated for the Christmas Holiday Season. It is truly magical!


Trip Highlights:

  • Explore Chinatown, Little Italy, Soho, Greenwich Village
  • See the Rockefeller Center Christmas tree and ice skating rink and visit NBC studios.
  • Walk through Central Park, see Times Square and a Broadway Show
  • Tour Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island, the Empire State Building and United Nations
  • Visit Ground Zero, Wall Street and the Financial District

Christmas time in New York is like no other city in the world!  Come see the city that never sleeps all dressed up for the holiday season. 

Take a stroll down 5th Avenue while dodging holiday shoppers and see the elaborate window displays of stores like Tiffany’s,  Saks and Gucci. 

Visit the Christmas tree at Rockefeller Center and look down at the people below as they skate around the famous rink and visit NBC studios. 

Do some holiday shopping at Macy’s, Bloomingdales and Niketown.  Gaze at the bright lights of Times Square after dinner as you exit the Hard Rock Café with your group and then take in a live Broadway show.  It’s Christmas time in the city, experience the magic!
