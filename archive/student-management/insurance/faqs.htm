 Do I need to use certain doctors or hospitals under my plan?

No. You can go to any doctor or hospital, however it is more likely that your insurance plan will be accepted if you use a provider that is affiliated with the Plan. Go to the following link: HEALTH CARE PROVIDER DIRECTORY for a listing of physicians in your area.  

Call 1-888-753-1000 to speak with a Customer Service Representative or contact Coordinated Benefit Plans by email for further details.

How can I contact the Claims Office about a claim?

Coordinated Benefits provides online access to Claims information for registered participants (to learn more about registering for the site, go to Medi-Web Log in). You may also call 1-888-753-1000 to speak with a Customer Service Representative or contact us by email, being sure to reference your name, affiliation with SMG and the date of injury/loss.

How do I submit (file) a claim?

Please refer to the claim forms and submission information page on this site. Claim forms should be completed fully, and only original bills are accepted. Printed claim forms may be sent to Co-ordinated Benefit Plans, Inc.  P.O. Box 26222  Tampa, FL 33623-6222 , or by fax to (727) 799-9093.

- Top of Page


How long will it take for my claim to be paid?

Co-ordinated Benefit’s goal is to process all claims within 4 business days of receipt of the claim.

My claim was denied. Is there a grievance procedure?

Within 60 days, you may request, in writing, a review of any denied claim. All requests for review must specify why you believe the denial was made in error. Whenever possible, requests should attach supporting documentation (for example, medical bills and/or correspondence.) Send correspondence to: Co-ordinated Benefit Plans, Inc.  P.O. Box 26222  Tampa, FL 33623-6222 , or by fax to (727) 799-9093. We respond to such requests within 10 business days of receipt.
