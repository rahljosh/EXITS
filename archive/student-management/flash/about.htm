Student Management Group was founded in 2003.  It was founded in an effort to provide management assistance and leadership to interested student foreign exchange organizations.

Currently, SMG assists in the supervision and management of the following organizations:

- International Student Exchange
- IntoEdventures
- ASA International
- DM Discoveries
- CSB International

These companies are all experts in the placement of foreign students in American public and private high schools.  SMG also assists in the in the Work and Travel programs as well as the Trainee Programs for university students.  It provides the experience, management and qualitative leadership that have quickly moved SMG and the companies for which it assists to the top of the exchange industry.  Its emphasis on quality performance among everyone involved in the exchange experience has made SMG unique among its competitors.  The quantitative growth of SMG each year is only one indicator of its qualitative nature.  Excellence in performance and commitment to the students, families and schools are even greater and more worthwhile indicators.      

SMG has recently increased its assistance and leadership to its specialized services including, AYP in Canada, PRE AYP English Camps, Outbound for American Students and the SMG Tour Programs in the States for all exchange students.

The ability of the management team of SMG, to bring, lead and assist various companies in these wonderfully and uniquely designed programs, continues to gain acclaim not only in the United States but also throughout the world.  International Representatives, as well as schools, students and families have become accustomed to the service and quality of SMG-assisted programs.

SMG knows that working together we can make a difference.
