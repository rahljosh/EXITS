Private High School Program (F-1 Visa Program)

  The Private High School Program is an F-1 visa program for high school aged, international students.  The program was created to give international students choice and flexibility in their educational options.  Students are able to choose the school to which they wish to apply from a list of schools carefully prepared by the private high school staff.  The program’s goal is to offer high-level academic options while also ensuring that each student who applies to the program is placed in a high school that will meet their needs and help them accomplish their long-term goals.
