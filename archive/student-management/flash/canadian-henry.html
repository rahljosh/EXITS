Features & Programs
The school was built in 1961 and is situated on 13.77 acres. A recent modernization of the school upgraded many of our facilities that now include a Library and research centre, several networked
computer labs, large bright classrooms, 3 gymnasiums, a drama room, a band room, a dance studio and a Fitness Centre.

    • One of the premier high schools in the province of Alberta
    • Fully networked with internal wireless access
    • Outstanding Library and Research Centre
    • International Baccalaureate Program (IB)
    • Gifted and Talented Education Program (GATE)
    • Full range of Academic Programs
    • International Students Program
    • Diploma and IB exam results consistently above City and
       Provincial Standards
    • Fine Arts Program includes: Advanced Acting, Art, Band, Choir,
       Dance, Drama, Design Studies and Multi-Media
    • Diverse Programs which include Career and Technology
       Studies (CTS), Second Languages (Spanish and French),
       Physical Education, ACCESS, Paced Learning Program (PLP),
       ESL & ESL Literacy, and Learning Strategies
    • Division I Athletic Teams in all sports
    • Fitness Centre for students and staff
    • Full range of athletic and extra-curricular programs




Extracurricular Activities
Student Council is the voice of the student body. The School exists to provide an enjoyable, friendly environment within the school that allows students to feel proud to attend Henry Wise Wood. Projects are numerous and varying. From fundraisers to dances to running the Warrior Shop, Student Council is always involved when it comes to student led activities. It is also a way in which students can change the school for the better.

School’s members constantly strive to be the best, be it a simple gingerbread house competition or a clothing drive for the homeless. Most importantly Henry Wise Wood is an organization that makes a difference- in the school, community and world.
