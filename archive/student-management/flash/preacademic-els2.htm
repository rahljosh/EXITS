Pre-Academic Year ESL Program for J-1 visa high school students

Locations: Southern California and Boston
Arrival date: July 16th
Start date: July 17th
Departure date to AYP Family: Saturday, August 12th
( For 4 weeks participants)
In order to participate in the PRE-AYP program, notification must be given at the time that the application is received by SMG.

Program includes:
  - 25 hours per week of professional ESL instruction
  - Availability of college facilities
  - Insurance coverage
  - Complete homestay: 2 students per family
  - 2 meals provided weekdays and 3 meals on weekends

  - Use of textbooks
  - Internet service on campus
  - Airport pick-up and drop-off
  - Supervised excursions (extra fee)

   Prices: (NET)  
For further information about the prices, please contact Sasha at sasha@student-management.com

  Program duration may vary depending on scheduled date of placement in host family. If student must leave program prior to August 12th money will be pro-rated and refunded/credited. Every extra day after August 12th in host family is $20 per day, every extra day with lessons add another $20 per day.

  No additional application is necessary. Student can participate in this program with regular J-1 visa.
  No other visa is necessary. Non AYP students are also eligible.

STUDENTS CAN CHOOSE:
  • Dean College, Franklin, Massachusetts– Boston/Logan Airpot
  • Citrus College, Glendora, California– Lax Airpot
  • Oxnard College, Oxnard, California– Lax Airpot
  • Mira Costa College, Oceanside, California– San Diego Airpot
  • Lock Haven University, Lock Haven, Pennsylvania– State College Airpot
