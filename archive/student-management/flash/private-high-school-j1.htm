Private High School Program (J-1 Visa Program)

The J-1 Private High School Program was created in an effort to offer a choice to international students. Private schools, in the United States, offer excellent educational opportunities for American and international students alike. Some of the finest high schools and educational institutions are working with SMG on this program. J-1 Private Schools offer this opportunity at a lower rate of tuition. Also, the Private Schools used in this program, are not able to offer an I-20.

The four SMG Companies that offer the J-1 Private program are:
• ASA International
• DM Discoveries
• ISE - International Student Exchange
• IntoEdVentures
