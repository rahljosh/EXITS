H2B VISA PROGRAM

The SMG H2B Visa Program is available to both students and non-students and offers to young adults the opportunity to work and live temporarily in the United States. The maximum program duration is 10 months while the average program typically lasts 6 to 7 months.

The Program is designed to allow non-professional, non-U.S. citizens to work specifically for one company (employer), on a basic skills positions, fulfilling their seasonal and/or peak staffing needs. The H2B type of visa is not guaranteed through the company (employer), but is subject to Department of Labor, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services and U.S. Consulate approval.

The SMG H2B Visa Program includes:
  Elegibility
▪ Age 19-40
▪ High School Diploma
▪ Intermediate level of English
  Length
▪ Maximum 10 months
▪ Extension up to a maximum of 3 years
  Position areas
▪ Resorts and Amusement parks
▪ Hotels and Casinos
▪ Restaurants and fast-food stores
▪ Landscaping companies
▪ Mobile ice cream companies
  Placement location
▪ Throughout the U.S.
• Program participants will have guaranteed and secured work placement before arrival.

• Program participants will receive a wage that reflects the average rate being paid to the United States workers in the same industry and position. Also, program participants will be assisted in finding housing.

• Program participants will have all their H2B Visa documentation coordinated by SMG. This includes, but is not limited to, processing the Department of Labor certification application and the H2B Visa petition to the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services.

• Program participants will be enrolled in a comprehensive travel insurance plan for the duration of their stay in the U.S.

• Program participants will be given a pre-departure orientation in their home country.

• Program participants will be supplied with a program identification card, a program handbook , a 24-hour support phone number and access to tax refund services.
