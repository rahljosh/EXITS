St. Francis Xavier High School
Mission
“Caritas Christi” (The Love of Christ). St. Francis Xavier High School has always had a tradition of excellence academically as well as athletically.
This success is the result of cooperation and support from home as well as the dedication and hard work on the part of the staff.

Features
    • St. Francis Xavier is designated as a Sports Academy School
       and an International Language School
    • Comprehensive academic high school program, including an
       Honors Advanced Placement and French Immersion program
    • Sport, fitness and lifestyle program including sport
       development academies
    • Safe and caring Catholic learning environment for 1,100
       students

Programs
    • Advanced placement academic program
    • Honors program
    • Modified French Immersion program
    • Sports Development Academies in Soccer, Lacrosse, Hockey,
       Baseball and expanding in other Sports
    • Fitness and training center with full time Fitness& Lifestyle
       Instructor
    • Independent learning opportunities available to students
       based on academic and program needs
    • Comprehensive technology integration across the curriculum
    • Community service requirements for all students


Extracurricular Activities
Peer Support, Choir, Prayer Group, Grad Committee, Drama Club, Travel Club, Fitness Club, F.X. Improv Team, Ski Club, Intramurals, Social Justice, French/Italian/Spanish Club, Speech & Debate Club, Leadership Team, Yearbook, Newspaper Club, 25 competitive sports teams, Badminton, Rugby, Basketball, Soccer, Curling, Cross Country, Swimming, Golf, Football, Track & Field, Volleyball, Cheer Team, Sport Development Programs - Hockey, Soccer and Lacrosse, FX Live TV.
