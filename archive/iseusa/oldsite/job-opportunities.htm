You can help bring the world to your school and community by becoming an ISE Area Representative. Help us locate, screen and orientate volunteer host families who live within 100 miles of your home. Secure school permission for students to attend local high schools. Help to resolve any problems which may occur.

As an independant contractor for ISE, you will receive a stipend for your placements and student supervision.

But, most of all, ISE Area Representatives allow families, schools and communities to join ISE in our mission to "educate tomorrow's leaders."
ISE will provide training and support to all Area Representatives through our network of Regional Advisors and Regional Directors. You may also qualify for our annual overseas trip by placing 7 students.

ISE offers a stipend for each student who is successfully placed and supervised. In addition, for Area Representatives ISE offers an incentive travel program. In November of 2008, ISE will bring qualifiying reps to Athens, Greece.
