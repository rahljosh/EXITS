:: About DMD ::

  DM Discoveries (DMD) has been successfully operating exchange programs for high school students since 1997. In addition to operating the Private High School Program DMD operates a J-1 visa high school exchange program.

  DMD helps more than 400 students attend private schools each year. The DMD main office is located in Babylon, NY. The Private High School Program staff is ready to assist students with any of their educational or cultural needs.




  DMD is also part of the SMG Group. A group of co-related exchange companies that provide many other cultural and educational programs:

    · High School Program (J-1 visa program)
    · Summer Work and Travel (J-1 visa program)
    · Trainee Program (J-1 visa program)
    · College and University Program (F-1 visa program)
    · Foundation Program
    · Pre-Academic Year ESL Program for J-1 visa high school students
    · Pre-Academic Year Orientation Camp for J-1 visa high school students
