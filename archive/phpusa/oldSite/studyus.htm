:: STUDY IN THE US ::

The Private High School Program offers students, age 13-19 years old, the opportunity to study and live in the United States.

The DMD Academic Year Program (AYP 10) and the Semester Program (AYP 5) are designed to provide foreign students with an intensive cultural and academic experience in an American family or boarding environment.

DMD places students in all areas of the United States and has qualified School Representatives throughout the country to work closely with the exchange students, host families and private schools.

DMD also works with a network of independent international educational partners who provide information, screening and orientations for prospective applicants.

If you are interested in experiencing American life by participating in a DMD Program, go to the "contact_us" page to fill out your information and a representative from your home country will contact you.
