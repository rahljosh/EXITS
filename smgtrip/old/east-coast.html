See NYC, Washington, D.C., & Philadelphia
with SMG students from all over the world!
Explore the foundations of American history on this week-long tour of three major East Coast cities. Enjoy the preserved colonial charm of Philadelphia, the stately monuments in our nation's capital, and the eternally dazzling display of New York City. Skyscrapers that disappear into the clouds, distinctive shops and eateries, and uniquely American culture and hospitality highlight this tour.

Trip Highlights:

  • See the White House, The Capitol and the Lincoln Memorial in Washington, D.C.
  • Tour the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building and see the lights of Broadway
  • Walk through Central Park and explore Chinatown in New York City
  • Experience the history of the United States at the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall and all about Benjamin Franklin in Philadelphia.

See the capital city of the United States, Washington, D.C. Visit the Lincoln, Vietnam and the World War II memorials.  Tour the museums of the Smithsonian.

Take a group photo in front of the White House and the Capitol Building.  On to Philadelphia a city so rich in American history. 

Take a guided tour of the Liberty Bell and Independence Hall where the Declaration of Independence was signed.  In New York, the Big Apple, see the city from the top of the Empire State Building. 

Take the Staten Island ferry right past the Statue of Liberty for some great photos.  Visit Rockefeller Center and 5th Avenue.  Explore neighborhoods like Chinatown, Soho and Little Italy and take a walk through Central Park.  Join us on the East Coast Excursion, a true experience of American culture and history.
