Flight Quote Form Information

BEFORE PURCHASING AN AIRLINE TICKET PLEASE MAKE SURE YOU COMPLETE THE DEPOSIT FORM AND ARE CONFIRMED ON A TRIP.


DO NOT PURCHASE A TICKET UNTIL YOU ARE CONFIRMED ON A TOUR.

You may purchase your airline ticket yourself online, or with a local travel agency, or complete our FLIGHT QUOTE FORM online. Just make sure before you purchase a ticket it is correct with arrival and departure cities and the times are correct with a.m. and p.m. and the different time zones for arrivals and departure times and correct dates. No one should arrive a day earlier or depart a day later on any tours other than the tour dates listed for the trip choices on our web-site. Please notify us immediately if your flights are other than listed on the trip dates if you made any error choosing a flight. Do not fly in a day earlier to the tour!
