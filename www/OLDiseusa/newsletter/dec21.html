Single Person Placement

CFR Title 22, Part 62, Subpart B, §62.25 (j)(9)
Ensure that a potential single adult host parent without a child in the home undergoes a secondary level review by an organizational representative other than the individual who recruited and selected the applicant. Such secondary review should include demonstrated evidence of the individual's friends or family who can provide an additional support network for the exchange student and evidence of the individual's ties to his/her community. Both the exchange student and his or her natural parents must agree in writing in advance of the student's placement with a single adult host parent without a child in the home

In order to comply with this new regulation, the following sections have been updated:

  1. General Host Family overview.
  2. Placement Management Menu
  3. Paperwork Tracking in Placement Management
  4. Letters Section of student overview
The missing paperwork menu will function the same as before, there will just be 3 additional forms that are needed prior to all paperwork being complete.

The paperwork icon, , will continue to indicate red when forms are missing, corresponding to either SPP or non-SPP needs.


The link from the letters section will send the SPP letter to the International Agent. SIgnatures from Natural Parents, International Agent, Student and are required.








2nd Host Family Visit

CFR Title 22, Part 62, Subpart B, §62.25 (d)(12)
That a sponsor representative other than the local coordinator who recruited, screened and selected the host family visit the exchange student/host family home within the first or second month following the student's placement in the home.
Due to the need of a second host family visit, the paperwork section has been updated to indicate the need for this second host family visit. Due to the fact that this needs to be done by someone other then the placing or supervising rep, future changes may be made accordingly.
