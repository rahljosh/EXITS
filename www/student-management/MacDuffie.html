MacDuffie is a small school with a rich liberal arts curriculum directed toward a diverse college-bound student body.

The MacDuffie School has been in operation for 124 years. It is one of the leading college preparatory schools in the United States for international and American students. It is located in Granby, Massachusetts, 90 minutes from Boston and 2 hours from New York. It offers students a choice of homestay or dorm stay. The campus is situated in a beautiful setting on 500 acres of lush New England green in the heart of Western Massachusetts. The region is rich in academic, cultural and recreational opportunity with some of the most prestigious colleges and universities in the country.

Are You Aware of these Facts about MacDuffie?

  • It is a truly international school with over 100 Americans and 150 international students from 20 different countries
  • It offers a challenging academic curriculum, comprehensive fine and performing arts and a competitive and expanding athletic program
  • It has an innovative English Language Learner Program
  • It offers Honors and AP courses
  • 100% of all MacDuffie graduates are accepted at competitive 4 year colleges and universities
  • Residential options include dormintories, a small family-style residence, or off-campus homestay with host families
