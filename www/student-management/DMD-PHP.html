Providing a Personalized High School Experience

The Mission of the DMD Private High School Program is that many of the students that wish to experience and share cultures are given that opportunity. The DMD Private High School Program works with over 90 schools in the United States in an effort to help students obtain the dream of attending school and furthering their education in the United States in order to share their culture and obtain better English skills.

This Program has been designed for students who would like to advance their English skills and obtain a cultural experience. The DMD Private High School Program can accept students that are students ages 14-19, students of any Language or Academic Ability and students determined to graduate and attend a US University.

Sometimes parents and students will ask when would a student come and how long would they stay? The answer is simple and straight forward, we can make any program work for any students, but some simply guidelines are: students may apply at any time of the year, students can stay for 5 or 10 months and students can return to the same school for multiple years.


Many times in the beginning of the applying process, or even before a student wants to come they daydream about where they would go and what they would experience. We have schools located in 35 Stated across the USA. Many of our schools have a good sports team. Many of our schools are located near to the Oceans or big cities.

Natural parents, although excited about the prospect of great experience for their child can have reservations about the programs, wondering what type of experience the child will get and how safe the programs are. For the DMD Private High School Program, students may choose their own school, students can graduate and obtain an official High School Diploma, students can play sports and certainly student will improve their English and make friends and learn about the Culture of the host family and the United States.

The DMD Private High School Program has had over 11 years experience in placing students in host families and managing those students safely and effectively. The DMD Private High School Program There is a 24 hour service hotline and a local representative in place to answer questions from the natural parents and student at any time. The DMD Private High School program provides management services to the student for their entire stay in the program including health insurance, and by offering Social and Academic reporting monthly.
