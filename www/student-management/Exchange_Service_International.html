Welcomes you to "the F-1 Program in the Public Schools"

At Exchange Service International, we strive to increase cultural awareness by immersing our students in vibrant American communities with the freedom to choose the district in which they want to study. This attractive program allows students to share their lives with an American host family and find the district that will best fit their needs academically, socially and geographically.

Who Can Apply to the F-1 Public High School Program?

Students applying to the F-1 Public High School Program must be between the ages of 14 and 18 years old. Students must have completed 8th grade prior to arrival. Student must have a sufficient command of the English language and transcripts demonstrating passing grades. Students must also possess a high level of maturity and motivation, with the ability to adapt to American culture.

What Types of Programs are Offered?

Students can apply to the F-1 Public High School Program for a semester, an academic year or a calendar year, with both August and January start dates available. Students are primarily placed in host families, who have been thoroughly screened and selected by Exchange Service International. All families participating in the ESI program are paid families who are interested in opening their homes and sharing their lives with a teenager from abroad. These families understand the value of cultural exchange and the positive message that hosting an international student can bring to a local community. Select public school districts do offer boarding facilities as well.

When Can Students Apply?

The F-1 Public School Program offers rolling admission, however certain districts fill up fast so it is best to get applications in early to ensure first choice district. Please note that the student applies to a school district, not a particular high school. The district will then decide which high school best suits the student.

Where are the F-1 School Districts located?

The F-1 Public High School Program gives students the freedom to choose from over 40 Public School Districts in exciting locations all over the United States. Whether it is the beaches of Los Angeles, the bustling city life of Chicago, the outdoor adventures of Denver, or the academic atmosphere of Boston, the F-1 Public School program has something to appeal to all teens!

Why Choose the F-1 Public School Program?

  • Students have the ability to choose the school district where they will spend their exchange program. ESI offers many sought after locations in the United States including Boston, Orlando, Chicago, Los Angeles and many more cities to come.
  • Students with varied levels of English ability are accepted and will help the student to choose the right district to improve their language skills.
  • Applications are accepted later than most other programs, allowing students to apply as late as two months before their program would begin.
  • The entire process can be done online with our easy to use inline applications.
  • Staff will assist students to pick the school that would best fit their needs, therefore making certain that the school has the sport or club the student wants to participate in during their exchange program.
  • Students receive all of the same benefits of an American student, allowing them to receive official school transcripts for college admission and possibly even graduate at the end of their program if all criteria are met.
  • Students are placed with host families in their school communities. These host families are paid to host ESI students and are excited to share American culture and their family life with a student.
  • Host families are carefully screened and selected using background checks, a complete host family application, references and in-home visit to insure the student is in a safe and loving home.
  • Students are assigned a local representative who is always available to answer questions or address problems them throughout their stay. They visit personally with the student and complete monthly progress reports.
  • 24 hour emergency line is in place for students as well as a comprehensive health insurance policy.
  • Staff will support the student throughout the year to be sure all student goals have been reached before completing the year!
