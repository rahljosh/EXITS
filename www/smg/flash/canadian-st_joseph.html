St. Joseph High School and Ascension Collegiate
Mission
At St. Joseph High School, they pledge to empower all students for future choices and
challenges by helping them realize their fullest potential in a unique learning environment.
 
Features
    • A safe and caring learning environment permeated by the
       Catholic faith
    • Individualized Self-Directed Learning program unique to
       Edmonton area
    • A newly renovated and modernized educational facility for
      1600 students
    • Computer learning centers interconnected through state-of-
       the-art fibre optic network
    • Differentiated learning environment providing programs for all
       types of learners
    • Teacher Advisor assigned to each student to assist student
       with individualized education plans

Programs
    • Individualized Self-Directed Learning
    • Advanced Placement Program
    • Academic Support Program
    • English as a Second Language Program
    • Second Language Programs: French, Spanish, and Italian
    • Concert Band, Jazz Band and Choral Programs
    • Drama and Performance Arts
    • High Performance Fitness Centre



Extracurricular Activities
Yearbook, Student Union, Student Ambassador- Leadership Team, Intramurals, Rainbow of Hope - Social Justice Projects, Religious Celebrations, Grad-Prom Committee, Skills Canada, Fashion Show, Cross-Country, Track & Field, Volleyball, Basketball, Soccer, Badminton, Football, Curling, host school for Junior High Volleyball and Basketball Tournaments, Religious Retreats, Drama Productions, Concert and Jazz Bands, Citadel Club, Father Troy Classic Basketball Tournament.
