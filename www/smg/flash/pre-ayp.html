Pre-AYP English Camp

Locations: Southern California, Pennsylvania and Boston

Arrival Date: July 13th

Start Date: July 14th

Departure date to AYP Family: Saturday, August 9th (For 4 weeks participants)

In order to participate in the PRE-AYP program, notification must be given at the time that the application is received by SMG.

Program includes:
- 25 hours per week of professional ESL instruction
- Availability of college facilities
- Insurance coverage
- Complete homestay: 2 students per family
- 2 meals provided weekdays and 3 meals on weekends
- Use of textbooks
- Internet service on campus
- Airport pick-up and drop-off
- Supervised excursions (extra fee)


Program duration may vary depending on scheduled date of placement in host family. If student must leave program prior to August 9th money will be pro-rated and refunded/credited. Every extra day after August 11th in host family is $20 per day, every extra day with lessons add another $20 per day.

No additional application is necessary. Student can participate in this program with regular J-1 visa. No other visa is necessary. Non AYP students are also eligible.

Students Can Choose:
• Citrus College, Glendora, California - LAX Airport
• Oxnard Costa College, Oceanside, California - LAX Airport
• Mira Costa College, Oceanside, California - San Diego Airport
• Lock Haven University, Lock Haven, Pennsylvania - State College Airport
