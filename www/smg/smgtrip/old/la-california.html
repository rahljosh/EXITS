The sprawling metropolis of Los Angeles is as diverse as it is popular, and our California Coast tour is designed specifically to highlight the best of the area. Spend a day at Universal Studios. We’ll take you on a tour of Hollywood, Beverly Hills, and Rodeo Drive.

Trip Hightlights:                                

  • Disneyland.
  • Beverly Hills, Rodeo Drive & City of Angels
  • Universal Studios and Hard Rock Café

Come and have some real fun in the sun on this spectacular tour of Los Angeles.

Take a stroll on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, shop with the stars in Beverly Hills and visit a real live working Hollywood Studio. 

Spend an entire day at Disneyland “The Happiest place on Earth”.  Visit Universal Studios and the exciting “City Walk” where you will dine at the Universal Hard Rock Café. 

Jump in the surf at Venice Beach, and walk the famous Santa Monica Pier.  Tour Chinatown, the Fashion District and Olvera Street in downtown L.A. 

Grab your sunscreen and swimsuit and join us on this great tour of “The City of Angels”.
