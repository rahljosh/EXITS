Student Self Portrait
This "self portrait" painting is the work of Han Byul Ru, a South Korean exchange student who came to the US from Shenzhen, China where her family lives because of her father's job. Han is a Junior attending Grant Union High School in John Day, Oregon. Her art teacher is J. J. Collier. This "self portrait" was selected for publication on the front page of the local newspaper. In addition to Han's artistic abilities, she is an accomplished musician who plays piano, clarinet and sings.
