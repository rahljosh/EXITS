Have you ever thought about volunteering your time to a local charity or community organization? There are many different reasons for you to start volunteering:

  • To help others
  • To learn about an activity or organization that interests you
  • To gain perspective on life in the United States

In response to the continuing and growing trend of volunteerism in this country, ISE is offering a program of Community Service to their students. The program is called H.E.L.P. and provides an outline for students to use to experience a variety of service and enhance their American experience. H.E.L.P. stands for:

                H – Health and Human Issues
            E – Environment, Education and Emergencies
            L – Local Events, Festivals or Needs
            P – Politics, National Programs and NGO’s

Within those categories, students choose what interests them and participates in the service. We ask that the students complete from five to twenty hours of service, or as much as they want.

Your reasons for volunteering are as individual as you are, but no matter what your reasons you can get a lot out of volunteering. You can learn about yourself, learn about others and meet a lot of interesting people by volunteering.

Projects are being planned now and will run through April 2008. Contact your Area Representative for more information.
